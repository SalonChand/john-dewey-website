The People

Educators who know every child

Our strength is a faculty that treats teaching as a craft and each student as an individual.

Principal, John Dewey H.S. School

Our leadership team brings decades of combined experience in school education, curriculum design and child development to the daily life of the school.

Behind them stands a faculty of forty-six teachers, each committed to the belief that children learn best by doing, questioning and reflecting. They are supported by administrative, library, laboratory and pastoral staff who keep the school running with care.

Together, they make John Dewey not just a place of instruction but a community, one where every member is known and valued.
