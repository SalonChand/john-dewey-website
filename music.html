Music & Dance

Home / Co-curricular / Music & Dance

Rhythm, voice and expression

Music and dance give students a platform to express emotion and cultural heritage. We offer a balanced curriculum spanning traditional Nepalese and contemporary global styles.

Instruments and styles

  • Vocal Music: Classical, folk and contemporary choir.
  • Instrumental Training: Guitar, Madal, Keyboard and Violin.
  • Traditional Dance: Kathak and Nepalese folk forms.
  • Contemporary Dance: Hip-hop and modern creative movement.

Performing Arts Day

Each term, music and dance troupes come together for a grand performance, a celebration of discipline, effort and the joy of performing as one.

Musical advantages

  • Cognitive Growth
  • Disciplined Practice
  • Rhythmic Accuracy
  • Performance Skills
  • Team Coordination
Music studio

Equipped with modern instruments and recording facilities for young talent.
