Environment Club

Home / Co-curricular / Environment

Caring for our shared world

The Environment Club turns concern into action. Students lead recycling drives, plant trees and run awareness campaigns, learning that stewardship of the planet begins with everyday choices.

What we do

  • Tree Planting: Greening the campus and the community.
  • Recycling Drives: Reducing waste and sorting responsibly.
  • Awareness Campaigns: Educating peers on conservation.
  • Clean-up Programs: Restoring local public spaces.

Green Week

Each year the club leads Green Week, a series of planting, clean-up and awareness activities that involve the whole school in caring for the environment.

What students gain

  • Environmental Awareness
  • Civic Responsibility
  • Teamwork
  • Practical Action
  • Leadership
Eco initiatives

Ongoing projects keep students engaged with conservation throughout the year.
