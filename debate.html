Debate & Public Speaking

Home / Co-curricular / Debate

The art of reasoned argument

Debate sharpens the mind and steadies the voice. Students learn to research a position, structure an argument and speak persuasively under pressure, skills that serve them well beyond school.

What we practise

  • Formal Debate: Parliamentary and competitive formats.
  • Public Speaking: Structure, delivery and presence.
  • Research & Rebuttal: Building a case and answering the other side.
  • Mock Parliament: Simulated sessions on real issues.

Inter-school championships

Our debaters represent the school at inter-school championships through the year, returning with both trophies and a markedly greater confidence.

Skills gained

  • Critical Thinking
  • Persuasive Speaking
  • Quick Reasoning
  • Research Skills
  • Composure Under Pressure
Debate society

The society meets weekly to practise formats and prepare for competitions.
