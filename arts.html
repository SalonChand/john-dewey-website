Visual Arts

Home / Co-curricular / Visual Arts

Sparking imagination and creativity

The arts are a primary tool of education for the 21st century. We encourage students to explore their creative potential across mediums, from traditional painting to contemporary digital art.

What students learn

  • Painting & Sketching: Watercolour, acrylics and oil pastels.
  • Sculpture & Pottery: Form, texture and three-dimensional expression.
  • Multimedia Art: Traditional art combined with digital and recycled materials.
  • Art History: The cultural and historical impact of art across the ages.

Art exhibition and competition

We host regular exhibitions where students show their work to parents and the community, building confidence and an appreciation for diverse artistic perspectives.

Creative benefits

  • Self-Expression
  • Emotional Intelligence
  • Cultural Awareness
  • Fine Motor Skills
  • Confidence Building
Art exhibition

Our semi-annual exhibition showcases the finest work from every grade.
