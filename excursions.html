Home / Co-curricular / Field Trips

Learning beyond the classroom

Some lessons can only be learned on the road. Our field trips take students to cultural, historical and natural sites, turning the curriculum into lived experience.

Where we go

  • Heritage Sites: Temples, durbars and museums of the valley.
  • Natural Sites: Forests, rivers and conservation areas.
  • Industry Visits: Understanding how things are made and run.
  • Cultural Excursions: Festivals, crafts and living traditions.

Annual study tour

Senior grades undertake an annual study tour, a longer journey that combines learning, teamwork and the simple adventure of seeing somewhere new together.

What trips offer

  • Experiential Learning
  • Cultural Awareness
  • Independence
  • Teamwork
  • Lasting Memories
Safety first

Every trip is fully supervised, with trained staff and careful planning throughout.
