Sports & Athletics

Home / Co-curricular / Sports

Building strength and character

Sport is integral to our educational philosophy. Physical activity supports the whole child, building not only fitness but mental resilience, teamwork and leadership.

Programs offered

  • Football: Regular training and inter-school competition.
  • Basketball: A dedicated court and coaching.
  • Athletics: Track and field, sprinting, long jump and relay.
  • Indoor Games: Table tennis, badminton and chess.
  • Cricket: Team practice and seasonal tournaments.

Annual Sports Week

Each year the school holds its Annual Sports Week, where students from every house compete across a series of athletic and team events. It is a time of energy, spirit and healthy competition.

Program highlights

  • Professional Coaching
  • Inter-school Competitions
  • Leadership Opportunities
  • Health and Wellness Focus
  • Inclusive Participation
