About Us

A foundation for lifelong learning

John Dewey School was founded to carry forward a simple conviction: that excellent education, offered to every child, compounds over a lifetime.

Our goal is to help each student become a responsible, productive member of society, combining critical thinking, communication, collaboration and a fluency with current technology.
