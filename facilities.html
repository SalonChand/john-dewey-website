Our Campus

Spaces that support real learning

Every facility at John Dewey is built around a single idea, that children learn best by doing.

Library

A library built for readers

Reading is treated here as both a habit and a skill, and our library is its home. A bright, quiet space stocked with fiction, reference and periodicals invites students to read widely and often.

Extensive fiction and reference collection
Quiet reading and study zones
Guided reading sessions
Regular new acquisitions
Science Labs

Where curiosity is tested

Our physics, chemistry and biology laboratories give students the room and the apparatus to move from theory to hands-on understanding through real experimentation.

Dedicated physics, chemistry and biology labs
Modern apparatus and safety equipment
Supervised practical sessions
Project and research support
Computer Lab

Fluency with current technology

A well-equipped computer laboratory ensures every student develops genuine digital literacy, from foundational skills to coding and design.

Modern workstations with reliable internet
Coding and digital design instruction
Structured ICT curriculum
Supervised access for projects
Sports Ground

Space to move and compete

Physical activity is essential to holistic development. Our grounds and courts support football, basketball, athletics and a full calendar of inter-house and inter-school competition.

Football field and basketball court
Athletics track and field events
Indoor games facilities
Professional coaching
Cafeteria

Healthy food, shared tables

A clean, well-managed cafeteria serves balanced meals and gives students a welcoming place to gather, rest and refuel during the school day.

Hygienic, well-managed kitchen
Balanced and nutritious menus
Comfortable seating
Supervised meal times
Transport

Safe journeys, every day

Our transport service covers key routes across Kathmandu, with trained drivers and attentive staff ensuring every child travels to and from school safely.

Routes across the Kathmandu valley
Trained drivers and attendants
Regularly maintained vehicles
Tracked, supervised journeys
