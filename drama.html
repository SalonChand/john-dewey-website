Drama & Theater

Home / Co-curricular / Drama & Theater

Bringing stories to life

Drama is about more than acting. It builds empathy, an understanding of complex narrative, and real self-confidence, as students learn to own the stage and communicate with clarity.

Stages of learning

  • Acting & Role-play: Character development and emotional range.
  • Stagecraft: Lighting, sound and set design.
  • Script Writing: Writing and producing original plays.
  • Public Speaking: Body language and voice modulation.

Annual School Play

The Annual School Play is the highlight of our calendar, with broad participation across the school. Students manage everything, from marketing to the final performance.

Stage benefits

  • Public Speaking
  • Empathy and Understanding
  • Creative Problem Solving
  • Team Collaboration
  • Overcoming Stage Fear
