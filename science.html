Science & Innovation

Home / Co-curricular / Science

Exploring the frontiers of discovery

Every child is a natural scientist. Our Science and Innovation program turns innate curiosity into structured critical thinking and problem-solving through hands-on experimentation.

What we focus on

  • Experimental Labs: Practical chemistry, physics and biology.
  • Robotics & Coding: Building and programming machines for real tasks.
  • Invention Challenges: Designing creative solutions to everyday problems.
  • Astronomy Nights: Exploring the cosmos through high-powered telescopes.

Science Fair

Our flagship Annual Science Fair invites students to present innovative projects to a panel of expert judges, a day of real creative energy and mastery of the scientific method.

Why science

  • Critical Thinking
  • Hands-on Experience
  • Data Analysis
  • Future-Ready Skills
  • Natural Curiosity
Innovation hub

The Innovation Hub is open every afternoon for independent student research.
