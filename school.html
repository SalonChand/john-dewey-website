Academic Excellence

School level curriculum

John Dewey School follows Nepal's national curriculum with an eye on global standards. Through Grade IV, children learn without formal examinations or fixed textbooks, drawing instead on varied resources and regular, gentle assessment.

There is a high focus on reading, as both habit and skill, and the arts and music are held no less important than science or mathematics. We embrace diversity and ensure every student has meaningful time at school.

From one view, our curriculum is deliberately light and unhurried. That is because we value the process of learning as much as the content. Students do not learn a topic in order to reproduce it in an exam; they learn it in order to understand.

Admissions

Eligibility for enrolment

We are generally non-selective, because academic performance alone does not tell us everything about a child.

We do not require a formal exam or interview, and enrol students on a first come, first served basis. We do, however, check that the school will be a good fit for the child and the family.

Age Requirement

The learner should be five years old by June 1 of the calendar year.

Transfer Certificate

No student from another school is admitted without a transfer certificate.

Previous Grade

Students must have completed the previous grade or provide equivalent documentation.

Entrance Test

No entrance exam for Grade 1 to 3. An aptitude test applies for Grade 4 and above.

Documents

The learner's birth certificate and other necessary academic records.

Admissions Open

Admission open for Grade 1 to 9
