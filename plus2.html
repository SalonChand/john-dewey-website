Stream Detail

Plus 2 Management

Our Grade XI and XII Management program is accredited by the National Examination Board and stands as a destination for students seeking a thorough management education.

Across two years, students acquire the technical knowledge for successful careers while cultivating the leadership qualities of effective managers, innovative entrepreneurs and esteemed professionals.

The curriculum is crafted to foster critical thinking, strategic decision-making and a real understanding of the complexities of management.

Stream Detail

Plus 2 Humanities

The Humanities program is a foundation for students aspiring to undergraduate study in Mass Communication, Sociology, English and related disciplines.

This two-year program spans Language Studies, Mass Communication, Sociology, Psychology, Economics and Business Studies, giving students a genuinely holistic education.

Cultural events, guest lectures and field trips enrich the academic experience and foster a vibrant intellectual community.
