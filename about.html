Who We Are

An education rooted in experience

John Dewey School was established with the hope of ensuring the longevity of excellent academic achievement through a commitment to quality education for all.

We take our name seriously. Learning here is active, not passive, children build understanding by doing, questioning and reflecting, supported by teachers who know them by name.

The result is a school where academic rigour and genuine warmth are not in tension, but reinforce one another.

Our Purpose

Mission and vision

Mission

To help each student become a responsible, productive member of society, equipped with critical thinking, clear communication, collaborative spirit and current technological skill.

Vision

To be a school where every child experiences meaningful learning, embraces their natural self, and leaves prepared to contribute to their community and country.

Team Members

A message from the Principal

Principal, John Dewey H.S. School

For sixteen years, our work has been guided by a belief that children flourish when they are trusted, challenged and cared for in equal measure.

We resist the temptation to reduce education to examination results alone. Reading is treated as both a habit and a skill; the arts and music are held no less important than science or mathematics.

To every parent considering our school, I extend a simple invitation: visit us, observe a class, and see whether John Dewey is the right home for your child's learning.
