Literary Club

Home / Co-curricular / Literary Club

A love of words, well kept

Reading is at the heart of this school, and the Literary Club is where that love deepens. Through book discussion, creative writing and recitation, students discover the pleasure and power of language.

What we explore

  • Book Discussions: Shared reading across genres and ages.
  • Creative Writing: Stories, poetry and personal essays.
  • Recitation: Poetry and prose performed aloud.
  • School Magazine: Student writing brought to print.

Literary Week

Literary Week brings the club to the whole school, with writing competitions, author talks and a celebration of the year’s best student work.

What reading builds

  • Vocabulary and Fluency
  • Imagination
  • Empathy
  • Clear Writing
  • Independent Thought
The reading room

A quiet corner of the library is reserved for the club’s weekly meetings.
